What is SolarSENSE?

SolarSENSE is an ASU student project to bring low cost soil sensor technology to rural smallholder farmers.  It is an off-grid solution that does not require electrical grid or mains power, as it is solar powered.  No internet connection or cloud computing connection is needed, as the micro-server is offline and collects the sensor data via Bluetooth or WiFi.   The SolarSENSE project's aim is to help rural smallholder farmers to better understand their soil conditions and adapt to climate change conditions.  This information can be used to improve regional and local food security and at the same time conserve the environment and biodiversity.
